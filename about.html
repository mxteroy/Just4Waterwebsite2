Our History

The Beginning

Just4Water grew from the aspirations of two Texas A&M college students, Guillermo Gomez-Salas and Mark Membreño, who had a passion for helping developing countries reduce the issue of water scarcity. With the support of other college students and professors, J4W was founded in January 2014 and built their first water well in Paso Real, Nicaragua on March 20, 2014.
